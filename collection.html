About the product

Our AltAir is simplisticly engineered to offer you the ultimate balance between convenience and an excellent listening experience.
All of this while being smaller than your palm.
Compatible on both iOS, Android and devices with Bluetooth.

Convenience in your palm

Our AltAir Vanilla comes in a small form factor so you don't have to worry about finding a place big enough to fit it.
Pairing to your device is easier than ever with just a flick of the lid, no fuss needed.
You won't have to worry about battery life with each bud lasting for up to 4 hours, and the case offering an additional 24 hours of charge. Unless you listen to music 24 hours a day, you can easily get by 2 days without needing to charge.

Immerse yourself

Housed inside each of the earbuds, which fit snuggly in your ear, are powerful drivers that offers clean and clear sound along with deep punchy bass. Perfectly suited for every genre of music, podcasts and general listening.
Bluetooth 5.0 means better range and a more stable connection so you never have to deal with connectivity issues.

EQ can be changed by user if preferred.

Control at your fingertip

With built in sensors, you are able to control your listening experience without the need to bring out your phone. It knows when to pause when they're taken out and knows when to play again when you put them back in. Tap controls on the side of the buds means you can freely accept calls, change or pause songs and access Siri all without needing to bring out your device.

For iOS only. Tap controls can be customised in setting.

Feature packed

Now comes with Audio Share, meaning you can now easily join your friend in listening to a song, podcast or any other audio stream together and be connected.
Live Listen allows you to use your device as a remote microphone to send sounds it picks up to your AltAir Vanilla. This is useful when you want to hear a conversation in a noisy room or hear someone speaking across the room.
Also comes with wireless charging so you can simply place it on top of a wireless charger.

Live Listen and Audio Share for iOS only. No iCloud Connect.

Get yours now

Why wait? With a device like this you can make your listening experience that much better with amazing portability and great sound while being packed full of features for convenience. Get yours today.
